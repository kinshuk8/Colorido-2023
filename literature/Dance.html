Dance

Do you breathe and live in dance!!!
R u the person to whom needs of dance matter.
Welcome to people who tap their feet, tune their beat and visualize the moments of life.
Perform and prove yourself you have a stage awaiting for you folks.

Specific Rules
  • No cinematic songs are allowed.
  • Duration of performance is 4 minutes. Exceeding the time may lead to deduction in marks.
  • The contestant should be dressed appropriately during the performance. Indecent exposure is NOT allowed.
  • The Participants are requested to bring two Pen Drives to avoid technical disturbance.
  • Any obscene/ offensive music/ song is not allowed.
  • The Participant may submit only one song with registration. The category for the song has to be selected during the registration.
  • Judgment is based on the basis of rhythm, formation, expression, costumes, make-up, sets on the overall effect.
  • Should make your own arrangements for makeup, costumes, etc.
  • If there will be condition arise of tie then a Battle round will happen. A random song will be asked by judges to dance for final judgment.
Cash Prizes :

First Prize Rs. 1,500/-

Second Prize Rs. 1,000/-

Third Prize Rs. 500/-

  • No offensive song will be used for performance.
  • FOR EMERGENCY it is advisable to have a duplicate PD or Flash Drive available.
  • Group Participants should be minimum of 4 & maximum of 6 participants.
  • Duration of performance: 5 minutes, exceeding the time may lead to a deduction in marks.
  • The participants in group dance should be registered individually.
  • Judgment is based on the basis of rhythm, formation, expression, costumes, make-up, sets on the overall effect.
  • Should make your own arrangements for makeup, costumes, etc.
  • If there will be condition arise of tie then a Battle round will happen. A random song will be asked by judges to dance for final judgment.
